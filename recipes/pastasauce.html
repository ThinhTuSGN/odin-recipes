Pasta Sauce with Italian Sausage

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of Italian sausage links
  • 1/2 ponund of lean ground beef
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 chooped onion
  • 1 chopped garlic
  • 16 ounce of canned tomatoes
  • 15 ounce of canned tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 bay leaf

Steps

  1. Removed casing from sausage links and cut into 1/2 inch slices.Cook sausage over medium heat until golden brown. Remove and set aside
  2. heat the ground beef, olive oil, garlic, and onion over medium heat until the meat is nicely browned and drain the juice
  3. Pour in tomato sauce; mix in salt, ground black pepper, basil, oregano, bayleaf, and cooked sausage. Simmer uncovered for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
